# Welcome to the Portfolio Platform Wiki

**Track, Analyze, and Optimize your investments with precision.**

This platform abstracts the complexity of managing multi-currency, multi-account investments into a unified, high-performance dashboard. Whether you are a passive index investor or an active trader, this tool provides the rigorous metrics you need to understand your true performance.

## üöÄ Quick Start

1.  **Create a Platform**: Go to **Settings > Master Data** and define the brokerage platforms you use (e.g., Questrade, Interactive Brokers).
2.  **Create an Account**: Link a specific account (e.g., "TFSA", "RRSP") to that Platform.
3.  **Add Activities**: Use the **Activities** logging form to input your history (Buys, Sells, Deposits).
4.  **View Dashboard**: Once data is entered, the **Dashboard** will automatically calculate your XIRR, TWR, and Net Worth.
